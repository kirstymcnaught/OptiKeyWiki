If you are unable to see the Menu key or Mouse key you may be running in 'Speech Only' mode (see ['Speech only mode'](https://github.com/JuliusSweetland/OptiKey/wiki/User-Guide#speech-only-mode) for more). To change from 'Speech Only' to 'Standard' mode;

1. With OptiKey focussed press ALT + M to open the Management Console.

2. Select the 'Visuals' tab at the top.

3. Change the 'Keyboard Set' from 'Speech Only' to 'Standard'.

4. Click the OK button at the bottom.
